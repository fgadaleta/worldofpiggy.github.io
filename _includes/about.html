Back in 2010 World of Piggy was a math webcomics of a pig sharing his room with an engineer. Piggy solved problems with math. The engineer executed. A terrific combination of skills!

Being at the center of applied mathematics, statistics, computer science and engineering the two forge ideas that implement in a plethora of domains, from finance to healthcare, social media and every day life.

Their major focus is on artificial intelligence, advanced data science and machine learning.
